Communication Plan

Outlines how information about the audit process, findings, and recommendations will be shared with stakeholders, ensuring transparency and engagement.

Communication Plan Details

A Communication Plan ensures that stakeholders are informed throughout the audit process, from initiation to final recommendations. It defines the methods, frequency, and audience for communication, as well as the types of information to be shared, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Key Elements of the Communication Plan:
  • Stakeholder Identification - List of individuals or groups to be communicated with.
  • Communication Channels - Email, meetings, reports, etc.
  • Frequency of Communication - Weekly updates, final report, etc.
  • Information Types - Audit progress, findings, and recommendations.
