Audit Objective

Specific goals and purposes that guide the audit process.

Audit Objective Details

The Audit Objective defines the goals for an audit, including the purpose, scope, and specific tasks. The objective provides direction for the audit team, ensuring that all necessary areas are evaluated to meet legal, regulatory, or organizational standards.

Example Objectives:
  • Compliance Check - Ensure adherence to industry regulations.
  • Risk Assessment - Identify and mitigate potential risks.
  • Control Evaluation - Assess the effectiveness of internal controls.
