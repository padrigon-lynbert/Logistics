Scope of the Audit

Boundaries and extent of the audit, specifying what will be examined, the time period covered, and the areas of focus.

Audit Scope Overview

The **Scope of the Audit** establishes the parameters and boundaries for the entire audit process, ensuring that the review remains focused, efficient, and aligned with the objectives. It defines what is included in the audit and what is excluded.

Key Areas of Focus
  • Time Period (e.g., fiscal year, quarter)
  • Specific Areas (financials, compliance, operational performance)
  • Departments and Functions
Exclusions

Items or areas outside the scope will not be examined. This helps prevent audit creep and ensures focus.
