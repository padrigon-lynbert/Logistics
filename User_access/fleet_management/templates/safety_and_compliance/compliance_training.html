Certification Process

  1. Complete mandatory training session
  2. Pass the final compliance quiz (score of 80% or higher)
  3. Receive training certificate (valid for 12 months)
  4. Attend annual refresher courses
Important: All fleet personnel must complete the compliance training within 30 days of hire. Failure to comply may lead to suspension of operational duties.
